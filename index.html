Quick Safety Guide

Simple actions for the most important weather alerts.

Safety
🌪️ Tornado Warning Go to the lowest level, interior room, away from windows.
⛈️ Severe Storm Warning Move indoors. Stay away from trees, windows, and open areas.
🌊 Flash Flood Warning Never drive through flooded roads. Turn around, don’t drown.
❄️ Winter Weather Plan extra travel time and watch for ice, especially bridges.

What Alert Types Mean

A simple breakdown for watches, warnings, and advisories.

Guide
Watch Conditions are possible. Stay weather aware and be ready.
Warning Danger is happening or expected soon. Take action.
Advisory Less severe than a warning, but impacts are still possible.
Statement Extra weather information that may affect plans or travel.
